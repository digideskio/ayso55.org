Shots from Trio Passing

 

OBJECTIVE:

This exercise requires the keeper to work on his footwork and stay "aware" of the position of the ball.

DESCRIPTION:

Three players should pass the ball to each other in front of the goal (just inside the penalty area). The keeper should side step back and forth to follow the path of the ball. Unannounced, one player will suddenly take a shot and the keeper must be in position to stop it. After he makes the save, he should bowl the ball back out to one of the players to continue passing to the other players.
