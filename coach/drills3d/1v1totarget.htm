1v1 to Target Players

 

OBJECTIVE:

This 1v1 exercise requires your players to check back, receive the ball, and turn quickly to attack an opponent.

DESCRIPTION:

Line up four cones in a square pattern. Two players should be lined up between two sets of cones facing each other. Another two players should be in the middle of the grid. A player on one end starts with the ball and passes it into the attacking player in the middle of the grid. This player must receive the ball, beat his opponent, and pass the ball to the player between the cones on the other side of the grid. The players on the ends must stay on the line.

VARIANT:

You can also use one side for the pass in and the other side as a goal with a keeper responsible for saving a shot by the attacking player instead of receiving a pass from him.
