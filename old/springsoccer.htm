Program Details:

Spring Recreation and Select programs are March 13 through May 8th. These U10-U19 programs are coach driven; meaning coaches must notify the region of their intent to coach spring and be approved. Then they may choose players to complete their roster. Should any opening remain on teams, players will be added from our interest list. Teams play within Area K which consists of Huntington Beach, Westminster, Fountain Valley, Long Beach, Santa Ana and Orange. There will be home and away games. Cost is $65 per player which includes a uniform. Guest players must pay $80 to cover player fees and insurance.

Recreation teams must consist of players who played in fall 2009 within our region. Up to 3 All-Stars or guest players are allowed. Divisions that play in rec are U10, U12 and U14. Competitive play is de-emphasized.

Select teams are more competitive in nature, usually consisting of All-star and/or Plus caliber players. No more than three guest players (non-region 55 fall season) are allowed, no exceptions.

ALL Spring Recreation and Select teams MUST have three referees listed and prepared to referee games for other region 55 teams at home. No team will be considered or accepted without referees to support our program. Any team not supporting our program with referees during the season will be removed from the schedule.
