Mail in Registration

Fall Registration after Final In Person Date

  1. Log into www.eAYSO.org.
  2. Click on PARENT button (in blue).
  3. Under “Sign up a returning player”, press Click Here (in blue)
  4. Click on player’s name you wish to register
  5. Verify all information is correct on next few pages.  If not, type in correct information and continue you have gone through player’s information.
  6. Click on I AGREE at the bottom of the last page.  (If you do not click on I AGREE, your player will not be registered for the current season.)
  7. After submitting the form, click on Click Here under the option Print my form or print another copy.
  8. You should see the player’s name with a printer icon after it.  Click on the icon and a pop-up screen will appear. 
  9. IN THE POP-UP SCREEN, click on “Click here to print” and print out 2 copies of the form.
  10. Sign both copies on page 1 and initial on page 2
  11. Mail the following:

a.     Both signed copies of the registration form (both pages signed and initialed)

b.     Copy of the player's birth certificate

 

Mail to:

AYSO Region 55

Post Office Box 1852

Huntington Beach, CA  92647

Attention:  Registrar

Your child will be placed on a wait list and will be placed on a team when an opening comes up.  You will be contacted by the region once your child gets placed.  We will collect the payment at this time.  After the teams are set, we will let you know that your child is still on the wait list.  This will happen at the beginning/middle of August.

Emails and phone calls are very heavy at this time of year so be patient.  We will let you know as soon as we can.

If this is your first time registering in eAYSO, click on First Time and then Parents button (in blue).  Input name and phone number on first page.  Then, input email address and create a password on the next page.  DO NOT CLICK ON “I’LL DO THIS LATER.”  Click on next and input all required information designated by a red asterisks (*). 

Print and mail forms as instructed in lines 7 through 11 above.
