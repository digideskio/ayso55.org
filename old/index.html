Our Mission


AYSO's Mission is to develop and deliver quality youth soccer programs which promote a fun, family environment based on AYSO's Six Philosophies:

  • Everyone Plays®
  • Balanced Teams
  • Open Registration
  • Positive Coaching
  • Good Sportsmanship
  • Player Development

Friends and Families of AYSO Region 55
Welcome to Fall 2013!

Whether you are brand new to AYSO, new to our region or have been with Region 55,
I’m confident you and your children will make new friends and have an enjoyable season!

With 1200 kids (and 600 volunteers!) in Region 55, Saturdays in the fall are filled
with the fun and enjoyment of the great game of soccer played with enthusiasm,
passion, and spirit only a kid can deliver! 

But it is not just the kids…coaches and referees are getting properly trained
and certified, team moms (and dads!) are planning fun-filled team activities,
volunteers all around are lending a hand to ensure when it’s time to roll the 
ball out for the first games on September 7th, nothing has been left to chance.
Indeed, we are all anxiously awaiting the start of the new season! 

But soccer doesn’t just start the first Saturday following Labor Day…a lot
is done well in advance. Practices begin in August, however work is done
year-round. Special thanks to the people on the Board of Region 55. They
have been giving their time, energy, and expertise to bring soccer to the kids.
From player and volunteer registrations to team formation to referee and
coach training to equipment and field safety to our treasurer to our Child
and Volunteer Protection Advocate and all of our division
coordinators - Thank You! 

I invite you to be a part, get involved, enjoy the moments, and remember
it is only a game played by kids!

Thanks and we’ll see you on the pitch!

Butch Ducharme 

Commissioner Region 55

commissioner@ayso55.org



 
 

 



2013 Region 55 Picture and Picnic Day is
SUNDAY, October 13th
Location: Central Park/ at the picnic shelter off Varsity/Edwards
 
 
Each team is assigned a 1-hour volunteer duty to man activities
and/or to help with pictures.
 
Team Picture information packets will be in the bins for coaches and team
parents to pick up no later than Saturday,  October 12th.  They will be
packaged by individual team. 
 
Each player needs to bring their form, filled out, with them on Picture day. 
Each player receives a photo package with their registration.
This package includes: digital 5X7 team photo, a digital 3X5 individual photo 4 wallet
size individual photos and a photo button.
Picture times are correct and the photographer will not run behind or wait for your team!!
Have your team players arrive 15 minutes before their picture time and don’t forget your banner!
Bring a ball if your players want a ball in their individual photo.
