It's time to turn over the reigns to our next Regional Webmaster.  This position is available immediately.

The requirements for the webmaster position are simple

  • You must be creative
  • You must use Microsoft Frontpage (very easy to use - if you know Microsoft Word then you can do this!!)
  • Training included!!

Now I realize that some of you might know other web editing programs, but here's the scoop - this web page must be easily transferable to the next volunteer webmaster after you, and FrontPage is the most popular, easy to use web editor.  The website also uses many FrontPage extensions (special FrontPage functions) that only work when you use FrontPage.

Note: if you do not have FrontPage the region will supply you with the software.

What are the job duties?

  • Post the soccer league schedules throughout the year
  • Be creative
  • Post the various news and events sponsored by Region 55
  • Have fun
  • Keep the various other pages in the website up to date
  • Attend a monthly meeting of the Region 55 Board of Directors (2 hours once a month, weekday evening) where you will gain additional facts that will be put on the website.
  • Probably a few other things I forgot...

If you think you might be interested, and would like to discuss this with me, please contact me and I'll be happy to review it with you.  Be sure to include a phone number so I can call you back.
