To register for Spring Soccer, please do the following if you registered in the Fall of 2005:
  1. Log into www.eayso.org .
  2. Click on MyeAYSO at the top left.
  3. Click on Player at the top.
  4. You should now see your player’s name.  Click on the player you want to register.
  5. Verify the information is correct in eAYSO.  If not, correct the information.
  6. Click on Submit at the bottom of the form.
  7. After submitting the form, click on Print Form at the top of the screen (in eAYSO).
  8. You should see the player’s name with a printer icon after it.  Click on the icon and a pop-up screen will appear.  In the Pop-Up screen, click on “Click here to print” and print out 2 copies of the form.
  9. Sign both copies and give to your coach with a check in the amount of $65.00 made out to AYSO Region 55. 
  10. If you are not yet connected to a coach, send an email to springcoach@ayso55.org.

If you have not registered in eAYSO, please do the following:

  1. Go to www.eayso.org and click on First Time (only do this if it is your first time).
  2. Follow the prompts to input all User information.
  3. After User information is in, input all the parents’ information.
  4. Then input all of the player’s information.
  5. Click on Submit at the bottom of the form.
  6. After submitting the form, click on Print Form at the top of the screen (in eAYSO).
  7. You should see the player’s name with a printer icon after it.  Click on the icon and a pop-up screen will appear.  In the Pop-Up screen, click on “Click here to print” and print out 2 copies of the form.
  8. Sign both copies and give to your coach with a check in the amount of $65.00 made out to AYSO Region 55. 
  9. If you are not yet connected to a coach, send an email to springcoach@ayso55.org.
