Ask the Ref Expert

Send an email to the Ref Administrator with your referee related question / comments.
If you are asking for clarification on a specific call you saw or heard about or maybe made yourself.  Please be as specific as you can with the details, who had the ball, where were they on the field, was the clock still running... you get the idea. The more information the better answer you will get to your question.
Just to be clear, no calls are reversed, we will investigate and provide feedback.
Thanks!
